Do you want to help us?

There are many foundations in which you can help. There are many foundations in the country that help thousand of pets. They Promote collective awareness by promoting sterilization, respect for life, NO to animal abuse and responsible adoption Below you will find more info about some foundations in which you can donate or give a hand. It would be great for the foundation and those beutiful animal will be grateful all life.

Fundacion Corazon Animal

Non-profit foundation that is in charge of sheltering dogs and cats from Medellin and the metropolitan area, in a situation of abandonment and vulnerability in order to find responsible and loving homes.

Fundacion Tepa

Animals that arrive at the foundation receive full veterinary care, including sterilization, vaccination, deworming, and any treatment necessary to guarantee their well-being until we can find them a home.

Animalove

At Animalove we are dedicated to rescuing, giving veterinary treatment and love therapy, in order to resocialize puppies and kittens that come from painful, sad situations and deserve the best things in life.
