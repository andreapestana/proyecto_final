Why is your dog your best friend?

On several occasions, the dog has been declared as man's best friend, because during its existence it has proven its loyalty, fidelity and even its ability to give its life for its loved ones. In fact, you can be sure that your furry companion will always be by your side, regardless of your presence, he will offer you his protection and give you sincere love.

Fundacion Corazon Animal

Non-profit foundation that is in charge of sheltering dogs and cats from Medellin and the metropolitan area, in a situation of abandonment and vulnerability in order to find responsible and loving homes.

Fundacion Tepa

Animals that arrive at the foundation receive full veterinary care, including sterilization, vaccination, deworming, microchip identification, and any treatment necessary to guarantee their well-being until we can find them a home that will care for them for life.

Animalove

At Animalove we are dedicated to rescuing, giving veterinary treatment and love therapy, in order to resocialize puppies and kittens that come from painful, sad situations and deserve the best things in life.
